Tech. Companies

Apple Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California, that designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. Apple was founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne on April 1, 1976, to develop and sell personal computers

Samsung Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd.is a South Korean multinational electronics company headquartered in Suwon, South Korea.Through extremely complicated ownership structure with some circular ownership it is a part of the chaebol Samsung Group, accounting for 70% of the group's revenue in 2012.

Nokia Nokia Corporation is a Finnish multinational communications and information technology company, founded in 1865. Nokia is headquartered in Espoo, Uusimaa, in the greater Helsinki metropolitan area.Nokia is a public limited-liability company listed on the Helsinki Stock Exchange and New York Stock Exchange.
